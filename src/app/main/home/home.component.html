Welcome to GEAR!

What is GEAR?

GSA EA Analytics and Reporting (GEAR) is a tool to provide commonly used strategic, business, and technical information to users throughout GSA in order to provide insight into the common direction for GSA IT and to provide working groups with data that they need to do their jobs.

Who Can Use GEAR?

Anyone logged on to the GSA network can access and download data from GEAR. Business and technical need to update data, send a request to GEAR Manager here.

How Do I Use GEAR?

By clicking on the tabs on the left, you can explore the various applications, technologies, and investments surrounding GSA. Using the icons on the top right of each table, you can choose which variables to view, perform advanced searches, and download data.
