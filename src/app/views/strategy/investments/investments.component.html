IT Investments

An IT investment is a resource expenditure to address mission delivery and management support. This includes the development, modernization, enhancement, maintenance, and operation of IT assets or functional groups in a production environment. This data is imported from Folio, a Capital Planning and Investment Control (CPIC) solution that helps Agencies manage their IT Portfolios and submit required data to the OMB IT Dashboard.
