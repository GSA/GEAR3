Technology Category Model

Hover: Hover over a circle next to a technology category to see its description. In the pop-up window, click the icon to see additional information about the technology category or click the icon to close the window.

Click: Left click on the circle next to a technology category to see the next level of the technology category. (Not all technology categories have another level).

Search: The search box below can search for any of the technology category display names and expand the chart to the nodes that match. Enter your desired term into the search bar and press Enter. You will get an alert if no matching term is found. Click the icon to clear the search box and reset the chart. Click on the icon to display everything on one page.
