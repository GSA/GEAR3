Welcome to GEAR!

What is GEAR?

GSA Enterprise Architecture (EA) Analytics and Reporting (AR), or GEAR, provides commonly-used strategic, business, and technical information. Use GEAR to gain insight into the common direction for GSA IT, and get the data you need to do your job.

GEAR combines authoritative sources of Agency data with helpful references between the datasets to make the data actionable and to provide a more holistic understanding of GSA and its operations.

Who Can Use GEAR?

Anyone logged on to the GSA network can access and download data from GEAR. Please address any data discrepancies with data owners, so they can update their respective data sources.

Use the "Contact/Feedback" dropdown menu in the top right corner of the page to ask questions about IT Standards or GEAR itself.

How Do I Use GEAR?

Click on the tabs on the left (or use the expansion button on the top left to expand the tabs), to explore the various investments, applications, FISMA systems, and technology standards used in GSA. Each section has an icon for more information on how to use that table.

To search, use either the “Global Search” box at the top of the screen, or the topic-specific search box near the top of each topic page.
