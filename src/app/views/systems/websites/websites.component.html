This report represents GSA's inventory of public-facing websites (i.e. accessible to the general public and not behind any authentication or firewall). This report can be used to better understand how the public views and interacts with GSA's business services, offerings, and information. This data is imported from the Touchpoints websites inventory as well as from the Enterprise Digital Experience (EDX) team's ecxcli website scans.
