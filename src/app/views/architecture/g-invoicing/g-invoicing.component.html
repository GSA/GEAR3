G-invoicing is a new system from the Treasury Dept. for handling inter-agency agreements (IAAs). The Enterprise Architecture Team, is tasked with mapping out the current state of IAAs at and determining how best to incorporate G-invoicing. These pages are not meant to give a definitive overview of this effort, but rather a glimpse into our current modeling process. Questions? Contact us at ea_planning@gsa.gov
